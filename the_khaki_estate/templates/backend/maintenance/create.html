Reporting Guidelines
Before Reporting
  • Check if it's a simple fix you can handle
  • Take photos if safe to do so
  • Note when the issue started
  • Check if it affects other residents
Emergency Issues
  • Gas leaks or electrical hazards
  • Water flooding or major leaks
  • Security breaches or break-ins
  • Fire hazards or smoke
Response Times
  • Emergency: Within 2 hours
  • High Priority: Within 24 hours
  • Medium Priority: Within 3 days
  • Low Priority: Within 1 week
