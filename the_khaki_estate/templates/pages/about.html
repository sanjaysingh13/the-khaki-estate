About Our Management System

The Khaki Estate Management System is a comprehensive digital platform designed to enhance community living through efficient management of maintenance, bookings, events, and communication.

Version 2.0 - Enhanced Features

System Overview

A complete digital solution for modern residential community management

Maintenance Management

Submit, track, and manage maintenance requests with smart staff assignment, progress tracking, and cost management features.

  • Smart staff assignment
  • Real-time status updates
  • Cost tracking & reporting
  • Photo documentation
Facility Bookings

Book common area facilities including community hall, swimming pool, gymnasium, and garden areas with approval workflow.

  • Multiple facility types
  • Approval workflow
  • Fee management
  • Availability calendar
Communication Hub

Stay connected with community announcements, event notifications, and real-time updates through our comprehensive communication system.

  • Multi-category announcements
  • Urgency levels
  • Targeted messaging
  • Attachment support
Community Events

Organize and participate in community events with RSVP management, event details, and attendee coordination features.

  • Event creation & management
  • RSVP system
  • Capacity management
  • Event notifications
Smart Notifications

Receive timely updates about maintenance requests, bookings, events, and important announcements through our intelligent notification system.

  • Real-time alerts
  • Customizable preferences
  • Multiple channels
  • Priority levels
Photo Gallery & Sharing

Share and view community photos, event memories, and maintenance documentation through our integrated photo gallery and sharing platform.

  • Community photo gallery
  • Event photo sharing
  • Maintenance documentation
  • Photo organization & tags

Advanced System Features

Cutting-edge features that make community management efficient and user-friendly

Smart Staff Assignment

The system automatically suggests qualified staff based on request category and expertise, ensuring efficient task distribution and faster resolution times.

Cost Management

Track estimated vs actual costs with variance analysis, providing detailed financial insights and budget management capabilities.

Performance Metrics

Monitor staff performance, completion times, and resident satisfaction ratings with comprehensive analytics and reporting tools.

Advanced Status Tracking

7-stage status workflow with automatic timestamping, providing complete visibility into request progress from submission to completion.

Mobile Responsive

Fully optimized for mobile devices with touch navigation, push notifications, and offline capabilities for seamless access anywhere.

Security & Privacy

Enterprise-grade security with encrypted data transmission, privacy controls, access logging, and secure authentication systems.
